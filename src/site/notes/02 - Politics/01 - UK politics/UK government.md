## All major debates

### UK constitution
1. Is the UK constitution still fit for purpose?
2. How have constitutional reforms since 1997 been a success?
3. Should either of the aforementioned be reformed?

### UK Parliament
1. Is parliament effective in fulfilling it's functions?
2. Is parliament effective in scrutinizing the executive?
3. Which is more effective in fulfilling it's functions: the Lords or the Commons? 

### Prime Minister and the executive
- Where does power lie within the executive?
- What makes a Prime Minister powerful?
- How important are the conventions of ministerial responsibility?

### Supreme Court, the EU, and sovereignty
 - What is the role of the Supreme Court, and to what extent can it be considered neutral and independent?
- How far does the Supreme Court act as a check on the powers of both the legislature and the executive?
- What are the aims of the EU and how far has the EU impacted on both UK policy making and the UK political system?
- What is sovereignty and where can it now be said to lie in the UK political system?
