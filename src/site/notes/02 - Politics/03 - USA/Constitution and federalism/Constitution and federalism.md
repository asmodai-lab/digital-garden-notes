## Misc

### Was the constitution every meant to be democratic?

Short answer? No, probably not.

- the founding fathers did not like democracy, it was not their end goal.
	- the goal was limited government/preventing a tyrannical one.
	- Democracy, then, was their method of achieving this
- although they were aware of democracy's problems, so looked for a balance
	- i.e. no tyrannical government but also stopping a populist moron being elected by a moronic population.
