## Individualism 

1. Explain what liberals mean by the primacy of the individual over any group in society. 
    
2. What is the difference between egoistical individualism and developmental individualism? 
    
3. Why do classical liberals adopt an egoistical view of human nature? How does this shape their view of society and the economy? 
    
4. How do modern liberals use developmental individualism to justify a more active role for the state? 
    
5. To what extent do you think individualism remains the core unifying principle of liberalism?

## Freedom and Liberty 
1. Explain how liberals define freedom in relation to human nature. 
    
2. Why is freedom closely linked to individualism within liberal ideology? 
    
3. What is meant by the idea that freedom is ‘under the law’? Why do liberals see law as necessary for liberty? 
    
4. Explain two ways in which liberals seek to guarantee individual freedom in society. 
    
5. Why do liberals disagree over how far freedom should be limited by the state?
