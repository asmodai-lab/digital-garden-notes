> [!info] This can be included in a discussion on the liberal view of society, human nature, and the economy.

## Summary
- freedom is the ability to think and act as one wishes
	- closely linked to individualism
- liberals believe that the individual should be as free as possible
	- but that it must be constrained to come extent
- society must be based on individual freedom of individuals to flourish

## What is it?
A belief in the supreme importance of the individual -> commitment to individual freedom.
