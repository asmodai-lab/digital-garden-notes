## Human imperfection
> [!summary] Summary
> • Humans are limited in many ways and are prone to make mistakes and unwise choices in life
> • They are intellectually imperfect and do not have the capacity to make sense of the world.
> • They have limited capacity in psychological terms, seeking security and familiarity.
> • In moral terms they are unable to distinguish right from wrong and will stray if not threatened with punishment
