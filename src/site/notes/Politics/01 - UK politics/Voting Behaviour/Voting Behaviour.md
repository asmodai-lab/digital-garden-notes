Voting behaviour is the study of figuring out how any person is most likely to vote before they do it. This can be based on two categories of factors:

- [[Politics/01 - UK politics/Voting Behaviour/Social factors\|Social factors]]: like class, race, age, gender, education, and region
- [[Politics/01 - UK politics/Voting Behaviour/Non-social factors\|Non-social factors]]: like national events, the leader of a political party, the media, etc
